template<typename T>
concept hyperion::concepts::Passable

Concept that is the disjunction of most of the requirements for std::semiregular Requires that that type be at least one of:

  • copyable
  • movable
  • a reference
  • a pointer

template<typename T>
concept hyperion::concepts::NotPassable

Concept that is the negation of Passable<T> Requires that the type is NONE of:

  • copyable
  • movable
  • a reference
  • a pointer
